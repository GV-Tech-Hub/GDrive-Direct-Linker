GDrive-Direct-Linker

A web-based tool for converting Google Drive sharing links into direct download links.

Overview

This tool simplifies the process of sharing Google Drive files by converting standard sharing links into direct download links. Perfect for educators, content creators, and anyone who frequently shares files through Google Drive.

Features

  • Dark mode interface
  • One-click conversion
  • Automatic clipboard copy
  • Instant download initiation

Usage

  1. Open index.html in your web browser
  2. Paste your Google Drive sharing link
  3. Click "Create Direct Link"
  4. The converted link will be copied to your clipboard automatically

Requirements

  • Modern web browser
  • Internet connection
